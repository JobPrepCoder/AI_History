1943 Critical
McCulloch–Pitts Neuron Model
Warren McCulloch & Walter Pitts · MIT

The first mathematical model of a neuron — establishing that networks of binary threshold units can compute any logical function. The founding document of neural networks and computational neuroscience.

NeuralFoundational
1950Critical
The Turing Test
Alan Turing · University of Manchester

Turing's "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" proposes the Imitation Game as a test of machine intelligence. Defines AI's central question for 70+ years and remains culturally dominant today.

TheoryFounder
1957Critical
The Perceptron
Frank Rosenblatt · Cornell Aeronautical Lab

First learning algorithm — a single-layer neural network that automatically learns to classify inputs. Marks the transition from hand-coded AI to data-driven learning machines.

1956Critical
Dartmouth Conference — AI Born
McCarthy, Minsky, Shannon, Rochester

The summer workshop that formally founded Artificial Intelligence as a discipline. Ten researchers propose that "every aspect of learning or any other feature of intelligence can in principle be so precisely described that a machine can be made to simulate it."

SymbolicFoundational
1986Critical
Backpropagation Popularised
Rumelhart, Hinton, Williams · Nature

The algorithm for training multi-layer neural networks becomes practical and widely understood. Enables the entire field of deep learning — every modern neural network uses backprop or a derivative of it.

NeuralML
2012Critical
AlexNet — Deep Learning Ignites
Krizhevsky, Sutskever, Hinton · University of Toronto

Wins ImageNet with 15.3% top-5 error — crushing the 26.2% runner-up. This 10.9 point gap triggers a global pivot to deep learning. Every major tech company begins mass-hiring ML engineers within months.

2017Critical
"Attention Is All You Need" — Transformer
Vaswani et al. · Google Brain

Introduces the Transformer architecture — replacing RNNs with self-attention. Every modern LLM (GPT, BERT, Claude, Gemini, Llama) is built on this architecture. Cited 100,000+ times.

Nov 2022Critical
ChatGPT — AI Goes Mainstream
OpenAI

Fastest consumer product to 100M users (2 months). Triggers a global AI arms race and makes AI a mainstream technology. Governments begin drafting regulation; every major tech company accelerates AI roadmaps.

1959Major
Machine Learning Coined
Arthur Samuel · IBM

Samuel coins "Machine Learning" while building a world-class checkers program — the first self-learning system to exceed its creator's ability. The term and paradigm now underpin virtually all modern AI.

ML
1964Major
ELIZA — First Chatbot
Joseph Weizenbaum · MIT

First natural language processing chatbot simulating a Rogerian therapist. Users form emotional attachments despite no real understanding — sparking the first debate about AI anthropomorphism that echoes through to LLMs today.

NLP
1989Major
LeNet — First Practical CNN
Yann LeCun et al. · Bell Labs

Convolutional neural network trained with backpropagation to recognise handwritten digits. Deployed by the US Postal Service to read zip codes — the first real-world deep learning deployment.

1997Major
Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov
IBM Deep Blue

First computer to beat a reigning world chess champion under standard tournament conditions. A cultural watershed moment — front-page news globally — that proves AI can surpass human expertise in complex strategic domains.

1997Major
LSTM — Long Short-Term Memory
Hochreiter & Schmidhuber

Solves the vanishing gradient problem in recurrent networks. LSTMs power speech recognition, machine translation, and text generation for 20 years — until Transformers supersede them.

NeuralNLP
2005Major
Stanley Wins DARPA Grand Challenge
Sebastian Thrun · Stanford AI Lab

First autonomous vehicle to complete a 132-mile desert course. Spawns the autonomous driving industry: Waymo, Cruise, Tesla Autopilot, and others all trace their lineage to this moment.

RoboticsVision

2013Major
Word2Vec — Neural Word Embeddings
Mikolov et al. · Google

Learns rich semantic word representations from text. Demonstrates that word arithmetic encodes meaning ("king"−"man"+"woman"≈"queen"). Transforms NLP from hand-crafted features to learned representations.

NLP
2014Major
GANs — Generative Adversarial Networks
Ian Goodfellow et al. · Université de Montréal

Two competing networks (generator vs discriminator) produce photorealistic images. Launches the generative AI revolution — deepfakes, synthetic data, and eventually diffusion models all stem from this idea.

GenerativeVision
2016Major
AlphaGo Defeats Lee Sedol
DeepMind

Go has more board positions than atoms in the universe. AlphaGo's 4-1 victory over world champion Lee Sedol was predicted to be at least a decade away. Move 37 in Game 2 stunned professional players worldwide.

BERT — Bidirectional Transformers
Devlin et al. · Google AI

Pre-trained bidirectional transformer achieves state-of-the-art on 11 NLP benchmarks. Transfer learning becomes the dominant NLP paradigm — pre-train on massive text, fine-tune on specific tasks.

1951Notable
SNARC — First Neural Network Machine
Minsky & Edmonds · Princeton

3000 vacuum tubes simulating 40 neurons with adjustable synaptic weights — the first physical neural network computer.

1965Notable
DENDRAL — First Expert System
Feigenbaum, Lederberg · Stanford

Identifies molecular structures from mass spectrometry data, outperforming expert chemists — the first AI to demonstrate superhuman performance in a real scientific domain.

1982Notable
Hopfield Networks
John Hopfield · Caltech

Recurrent networks with associative memory properties re-legitimise neural network research after the perceptron winter, directly inspiring Hinton's Boltzmann machines.

Neural
1992Notable
Support Vector Machines
Vapnik & Cortes · Bell Labs

Principled statistical classification using maximum-margin hyperplanes. Dominates ML competitions throughout the 2000s before neural networks overtake in 2012.

ML
2008Notable
CUDA Enables GPU-Accelerated ML
NVIDIA + Andrew Ng · Stanford

General-purpose GPU programming with CUDA enables deep networks to train 70× faster than CPUs — the hardware catalyst for the 2012 deep learning explosion.

2013Notable

Mnih et al. · DeepMind

Deep neural network learns to play 49 Atari games at superhuman level from raw pixels — no game-specific engineering. Combines RL with deep learning for the first time at scale.

OpenAI

DALL-E generates creative images from text prompts; CLIP learns joint text-image representations. Together they launch the text-to-image revolution — Stable Diffusion, Midjourney, and Imagen follow within a year.

Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback trains models to be helpful and harmless. The key technique behind ChatGPT and all modern aligned AI assistants.
